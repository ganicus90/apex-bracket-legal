Delete Your Apex Bracket Account

You can request deletion of your Apex Bracket account at any time directly from within the app. This page explains the process and what happens to your data.

How to delete your account from within the app

  1. Open the Apex Bracket app on your device.
  2. Sign in to your account if you are not already signed in.
  3. Tap the profile icon in the top-left corner of the home screen.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. Review the on-screen explanation, then tap Delete My Account.
  6. Confirm by tapping Schedule Deletion.
30-day grace period: After you confirm deletion, your account is scheduled to be permanently deleted in 30 days. During this period you can cancel the deletion by signing back into the app and tapping Cancel Deletion on the home screen banner. After 30 days, all account data is permanently removed and the deletion cannot be reversed.

Can't sign in to use the in-app option?

If you are unable to sign in (for example, you forgot your password and can no longer access the email you registered with), email us at apexbracket@yahoo.com from any email address with the following information:

  • The email address used to register your Apex Bracket account.
  • A short statement confirming you are the account owner and want to delete the account.

We will verify the request and complete the deletion within 30 days.

What data is deleted

When your account is permanently deleted (either after the 30-day grace period or by direct request):

  • Your Apex Bracket user account (email, display name, authentication record) is permanently deleted.
  • Your password is permanently deleted.
  • Your account is removed from all leagues you joined.
  • Your bracket picks are permanently deleted.

What data may be retained

To preserve the integrity of leagues you participated in, your display name in historical bracket records is replaced with "Deleted User" rather than being fully erased. This is so other members of your former leagues can still view final standings without broken references.

Anonymized records may also be retained where required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or backup retention purposes, for a maximum of 90 days after deletion, after which they are permanently purged.
